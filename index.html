The Problem

The knowledge is there.
Finding it is the problem.

Design Release Engineers in Automobile, Consumer Electronics, Aerospace, etc. spend a significant part of every day on indirect work — hunting across PLM systems, shared drives, legacy PDFs, and tribal memory for knowledge that already exists somewhere in the organization.

The cost is not just time. A single late-stage design change in automotive can cost millions in tooling and program delay. When institutional knowledge walks out the door, those costs multiply across every future program.

The Product

Contextual design intelligence, inside your CAD environment.

DesignFlowAI integrates natively into CATIA and NX via CAA C++ API — where engineers already work. A natural language interface surfaces relevant knowledge based on cursor position, selected geometry, and PLM metadata, so the right standard or lesson learned appears exactly when and where it is needed.

The system combines a general engineering layer (industry standards, DFM rules, material properties) with a customer-specific layer (company lessons learned, internal guidelines, proprietary standards). The engineering knowledge layer of the engineering domain ensure responses are structurally grounded — not just textually similar.
